About us

About 30 years ago some close friends taught me how to fly fish on the San Juan River in New Mexico. A few years later I started using a tool similar to the Fly Wizard to hold the small flies while tying and for storing them. As I taught my sons and later on my wife how to fish, I always attached a Wizard to their fly vest. While fishing with some relatives who were visiting I let them use one of my fishing vests and he noticed the Fly Wizard and commented on how useful it was since he hadn’t had one on his fly vest. So I made up some Fly Wizards and took them to my local fishing spots. What I found out was interesting, a high percentage of fisherman use either forceps or fingers to hold the fly while tying. After seeing my Fly Wizard they all said that’s a must have fly tool.
